Remote Dome Control System

Oregone Observation Remote Control Center
December 2014 - March 2015

I created an ASCOM compliant user interface to control the dome on the Robbins Robotic Telescope, located at the Pine Mountain Observatory. The Robbins is a 14 inch telescope used by the University of Oregon for astronomical observations and outreach.

LabKey-ExaCloud Integration

Oregon Health and Science University
July 2015 - Present

I am working integrating the LabKey Server software with OHSU's ExaCloud supercomputer. The ultamite goals of the project is to allow researchers to submit jobs to the ExaCloud and retrieve the results within LabKey's user interface.
